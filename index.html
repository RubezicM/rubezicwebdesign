About me

Hello, my name is Miljan and I am a web-designer and front-end developer based in Serbia! I've made my first steps into this field with the big help from the Krojačeva škola web dizajna where I learned great deal about web design and typed my first lines of code. After that I kept learning on my own, and driven by the passion for the web design and development, I went deeper and deeper into a world of web-dev!

I am efficient in using HTML,CSS (SASS,LESS),Major CSS libraries,Javascript (ES5 and ES6),JQuery,PHP,Wordpress,Gulp,NPM,NodeJS. I am also using Photoshop, Illustrator,Fireworks for design based tasks.

I am able to build creative and modern web pages from scratch,from the initial idea to the working product that lives on the web. I consider myself well organized,hardworking and creative.

When I am not geeking out about codding I enjoy moving my body by working out or doing random sport activities.. I am always open for the conversations about almost anything really so feel free to get in touch!
